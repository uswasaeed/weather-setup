Days with most rain

Which days had the most rain, and how much rain are we talking about?

weather |>
  select(date, prcp) |>
  arrange(desc(prcp)) |>
  filter(prcp >= 5)

Data Takeaway: The heaviest rainfall days in Austin brought more than five inches of rain in a single day, with the highest reaching 7.55 inches on Nov. 15, 2001.

Hottest days

What are the hottest days in history?

weather |>
  select(date, tmax) |>
  arrange(desc(tmax)) |>
  head(10)

Data Takeaway: Austin’s hottest recorded temperature was 112 degrees, reached on Sept. 5, 2000, and Aug. 28, 2011. Multiple hot days occurred in 2011, appearing three times among the top 10 hottest days.

Days over 100

Which years had the most days of 100+ temperature? i.e., group by year and count the number of days with 100+ temps.

weather |>
  filter(tmax >= 100) |>
  group_by(year = year(date)) |>
  summarize(days_100_plus = n()) |>
  arrange(desc(days_100_plus)) |>
  filter(days_100_plus >= 50)

Data Takeaway: In 2011, Austin experienced 90 days with temperatures over 100 degrees, beating out 2023 by 10 more days over 100 degrees.
